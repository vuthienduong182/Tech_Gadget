iPhone 13 is a phone that exudes class at first sight thanks to its luxurious design with flat design language and excellent finishing quality. High-quality aluminum frame combined with super-hard Ceramic Shield protective glass creates a phone that is both fashionable and extremely durable. The IP68 water resistance of iPhone 13 gives you complete peace of mind against all risks from water during daily use.

Apple equips the iPhone 13 with a pair of excellent camera sensors with extremely large aperture and sensor size, adding Sensor-shift OIS optical image stabilization. That is the reason for the two lenses to be placed diagonally 45 degrees, suitable for space for new sensors. As a result, the iPhone 13 camera has the ability to capture up to 47% more light, for stunning photos and videos.

Not only simply filming, Cinematic movie mode on iPhone 13 also allows you to create movies with cinematic quality. Now your iPhone can easily record videos that remove backgrounds, blur the background, and smoothly switch subjects and focus both before and during recording. In television videos, the character talking will be in focus and the other context will be blurred. The iPhone 13 can completely film like that in a professional way. iPhone 13 even offers faster adjustments, with just a few taps and swipes.
